What Is KASHU?

A strategy game where you build cities, balance budgets, and survive economic chaos — all while learning real financial skills.

In the wake of a global economic crisis, you're one of the few trusted to rebuild your region.
With limited resources, debt looming, and an unpredictable economy, your job is simple:
Build a thriving city without going broke.

From solar farms to smart cities, from debt traps to government bailouts — KASHU makes every decision count.

How to Play (2–4 players)

  • Start with cash, land, and a worker.
  • Buy zones, build infrastructure, manage your city.
  • Spin the Economy Wheel every few rounds face inflation, tax changes, or a sudden boom.
  • Upgrade workers, invest smart, and collect income.
  • Game ends when all infrastructure types are built.
  • Win by having the highest total asset value (Property + Cash – Debt + Bonuses).

It’s not just about money — it's about strategy, synergy, and sustainability.
